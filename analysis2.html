4 Analysis after combining ABC together

Combine the group A, B, and C into one group names group P. The following results are based on the comparison between group P and AH. Sub group naming rule: The Capital letter(s) means the group name, the number mean the total score level. For example, the sub-group AH1 means the observations in it think themselves have no psychological illness and the total score level is 1.
